Brief Overview

Founded in 2025, saw seven workmates establish this shop called I'm Just a Crepe, created primarily for the only purpose of making Science High School students say, "Oh Wow! So yummy!" Sweet and savory creeps are sold, lavished with love. And adorably cat-shaped ice creams licked and savored to their full.

It is sheer love for these entirely original, cute, and kind-to-the-pocket snacks that bring joy to taste buds and hearts. Everywhere is made using nothing but the best of raw materials to guarantee freshness and great flavor.

Our hope is for this stall to be a-journey's-end for every single student wishing to pamper their palate whenever break time rolls around for some sweet treats. Mostly fun- with-cheap, instagrammable food-the students and faculty are the target market.

We hope to merge creativity, teamwork, and foodie passion to make I'm Just a Crepe a big part of any school event. Every bite will be a representation of joy, thrill, and a longing to return for more! And on every turn, we wish to engage in creating not just a snack, but an experience-full of sweetness with every product we offer.

Vision

Our vision for this business is we will grow quickly and will make sure every crepe ice cream and cookie ice cream sandwich is worth every bite.

Mission

Our mission is to provide students with cold, refreshing ice cream that not only delights their taste buds but also helps them recharge and refresh their minds during challenging school days.
